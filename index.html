Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of the communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Panda

  • There are two main types of pandas – the giant black-and-white panda and the Red Panda.
  • The giant panda is native to China.
  • The Giant panda is a bear and is the world’s most adored and protected rare animal.

Interesting Facts about camel

  • Camels are social animals who roam the deserts in search of food and water
  • There are two different types of camels: Dromedary Camels and Bractrion Camels.
  • They are specially adapted to the life in desert.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bear

  • The first toy bear is believed to be made by German toy maker Margarete Steiff in 1902
  • Walt Disney produced the first colour cartoon film featuring teddy bears – Alice and the Three Bears – in 1924.
  • The first British Teddy Bear Festival was held in 1989 in London.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are Very Fast.
  • They are Incredibly Social.
  • Pigeons are Spiritual to Some Religions.
